ABOUT OUR STORY

Many years ago, in the tropical lands of Brazil, Souza's Ice Cream was born, a small family business with big ice cream dreams. The sweet aroma of fresh ice cream began to envelop the streets, as the Souza family passionately dedicated themselves to producing small but delicious ice cream units. Back then, each spoonful carried not only the refreshing flavor, but also the warmth and dedication of a family passionate about what they did. Over time, the impeccable quality and unparalleled flavor of Souza's ice creams won hearts and palates across the country. Demand grew, and the small company became a medium-sized powerhouse in the world of ice cream, always maintaining the artisanal touch and family affection.
MISSION

Delight the hearts and palates of our customers, offering high-quality artisanal ice creams that provide moments of happiness and celebration. At Souza's Ice Cream, our mission is to create ice cream experiences that become lasting memories.

VISION

To be the most beloved and nationally recognized artisanal ice cream brand, promoting joy and unity through exceptional products and warm service.

VALUES

Passion for Quality: We are committed to producing exceptional ice cream using the highest quality ingredients and artisanal techniques. Family in Everything We Do: We keep family roots close to our hearts, promoting a welcoming work environment and building lasting relationships with our customers. Innovation with Tradition: We constantly seek to innovate in our flavors and processes, always maintaining respect for the traditions that made us who we are.
